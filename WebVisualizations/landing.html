Summary: Latitude VS. X

The intent of this project is to analyze weather patterns, particularly in their relations to the equator. As a sample, the data of hundreds of cities was gathered for the purpose of this project.

In this project, four visualizations clearly show the relationships, or lack thereof, between weather patterns and the equator. The four subgroups examined were temperature, wind speed, cloudiness, and humidity.
